Convenience care and urgent care centers see you faster and cost a lot less than the emergency room.     Need help answering?

Why else would you go to a convenience care or urgent care center? (Select all that apply.)

Have a sudden injury and need a walk-in appointment
Need care when your doctor’s office is closed
Need a flu shot
Have a minor health issue, like a sinus infection or the flu



Exactly! Oops — all of these are correct answers.

Convenience care and urgent care centers are great options if you have a minor injury that requires immediate care — or if your regular doctor’s office is closed. Most offer extended hours, including evenings and weekends. They can treat minor health problems like sinus infections or the flu. Some convenience care centers are housed within retail stores, making them a quick and easy spot to get your yearly flu shot. And they can save you a lot of time and money compared to a trip to the ER.
